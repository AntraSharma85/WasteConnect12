About Us

Working towards a cleaner, greener and sustainable India.

🌍 Our Mission

Our mission is to reduce waste, recycle efficiently, and create a sustainable ecosystem by connecting individuals, collectors, and manufacturers together under one platform.

✨ Key Features

  • ✔ Easy waste request system
  • ✔ Category-wise waste collection
  • ✔ Leaderboard & reward system
  • ✔ Manufacturer partnership

📊 Our Impact

Together with our contributors, we have recycled more than 10,000 kg of plastic, paper, and e-waste. Every contribution counts toward building a greener tomorrow.
